About Us

  Ruk Rakaganno is a largely volunteer based, non-governmental organization that is funded by grants, donations, membership fees and other revenueâˆ’generating activities carried out by the organization itself.

  It was founded in 1975 by a group of environmentally-conscious individuals who were concerned about the rapid deforestation taking place in the country and wished to take measures against it.

  During this initial period, Ruk Rakaganno was largely an advocacy group, which sought to influence policy makers and private deforesters. Presently, alongside advocacy, the group partakes in hands-on projects. Over the years, these have included:

  • Rejuvenating degraded land.
  • Replanting on coastal areas , which were devestated by the tsunami.
  • Carrying out education projects with schools and communities.
  • Forming a women's environment centre to empower women in conservation efforts.

 Ruk Rakaganno is member-based with an elected committee, headed by a president. The committee has primary responsibility for decision making of policies and projects.

 Ruk Rakaganno welcomes and encourages those who wish to become members, who would work with the society in their projects and advocacy to protect and conserve the environment.
